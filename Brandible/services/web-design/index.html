Our Process

How We Work

From initial concept to launch, we follow a proven process that ensures your website meets your goals and exceeds expectations.

Discovery

We learn about your business, goals, and audience to create a strategic plan.

Design

Custom layouts and visuals that reflect your brand and engage your visitors.

Develop

We build your site with clean code, fast performance, and mobile responsiveness.

Launch

We deploy your site, test everything, and ensure it's live and performing perfectly.

Support

Ongoing maintenance, updates, and support to keep your site running smoothly.

What’s included

  • Custom design, mobile-responsive build, and fast load times
  • SEO-ready structure and metadata
  • Analytics and optional CRM/email ties
  • Accessibility considered (keyboard nav, clear structure)
  • Optional CMS and hosting/maintenance

Who it’s for: Local service businesses, shops, consultants, and teams that want a clear scope, one point of contact, and a site that’s built to get calls and bookings.

Typical investment: Most projects run $2,000–$10,000+ based on scope and features. We’ll confirm everything on a short call and send a clear proposal—no hidden fees, flexible payment.
