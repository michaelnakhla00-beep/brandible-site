Branding & Identity

Branding is more than a logo — it’s the foundation of how people perceive and connect with your business. Whether you’re launching something new or refreshing an existing brand, we’ll help you develop a visual and verbal identity that feels authentic and memorable.

What’s Included

A complete toolkit to launch or refresh your brand with confidence.

Brand Discovery & Positioning

Clarify your mission, values, and audience so your brand speaks directly to the right people.

Logo Design (Primary + Alternate)

Distinct logo concepts with flexible variations for print, digital, and social use.

Color Palette & Typography

Purposeful color and font systems that reinforce your story across every touchpoint.

Visual Style Guidelines

Direction for imagery, iconography, and layout so everything looks cohesive.

Voice & Tone Strategy

Messaging pillars and sample copy to keep your brand sounding consistent everywhere.

Business Cards or Stationery Design

Polished print-ready assets that make a strong first impression.

Social Media Profile Graphics

Profile and cover designs that align with your new identity across platforms.

Brand Style Guide PDF

Our Process

How We Work

From discovery to delivery, we follow a proven process that ensures your brand identity is authentic, memorable, and ready to grow with you.

Discover

We dive deep into your business, values, and audience to understand what makes you unique.

Strategize

We develop a brand positioning strategy that differentiates you and resonates with your audience.

Design

We create logo concepts, color palettes, typography, and visual elements that bring your brand to life.

Refine

We iterate based on your feedback, ensuring every detail aligns with your vision and goals.

Deliver

We provide all brand assets, style guide, and files you need to use your new identity everywhere.
