Branding is more than a logo—it's how people perceive and connect with your business. We help you develop a visual and verbal identity that feels authentic and memorable, whether you're launching something new or refreshing an existing brand.

What we offer

  • Brand discovery & positioning. Clarify mission, values, and audience so your brand speaks to the right people.
  • Logo design. Primary and alternate concepts for print, digital, and social—distinct and flexible.
  • Color palette & typography. Purposeful color and font systems that reinforce your story everywhere.

Our Process

How We Work

From discovery to delivery, we follow a proven process that ensures your brand identity is authentic, memorable, and ready to grow with you.

Discover

We dive deep into your business, values, and audience to understand what makes you unique.

Strategize

We develop a brand positioning strategy that differentiates you and resonates with your audience.

Design

We create logo concepts, color palettes, typography, and visual elements that bring your brand to life.

Refine

We iterate based on your feedback, ensuring every detail aligns with your vision and goals.

Deliver

We provide all brand assets, style guide, and files you need to use your new identity everywhere.

What's included

  • Brand discovery and positioning aligned to your mission, values, and audience
  • Logo design (primary and alternate concepts for print, digital, and social)
  • Color palette and typography system
  • Voice, tone, and visual guidelines for cohesive messaging and imagery
  • Brand style guide PDF; optional business cards, stationery, and social profile graphics

Who it's for: Small businesses, startups, and entrepreneurs who want a coherent, professional look—retail shops, service providers, consultants, and local teams launching or refreshing their brand.

Typical investment: Most branding projects run $1,500–$5,000+ depending on scope and deliverables. We'll scope on a discovery call and send a clear proposal—transparent pricing, flexible payment schedules, no surprise add-ons.
